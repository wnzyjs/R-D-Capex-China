# China Chemicals R&D CAPEX Dashboard

Prepared for: Nouryon-related chemicals screening  
Prepared on: April 17, 2026

## 1. Executive Takeaways

- The strongest public China R&D CAPEX benchmarks in chemicals remain BASF, Solvay, Clariant, Arkema, and now Nouryon's 2025 innovation-center buildout.
- The clearest disclosed investment numbers are BASF at around EUR 280 million cumulative in Shanghai, Solvay at more than RMB 4 billion cumulative in Shanghai, Clariant at CHF 45 million in Shanghai, and Arkema at around EUR 600 million cumulative in Changshu for an integrated production-plus-R&D platform.
- Public sources are usually good on facility type and technical capability, but weak on highly specific process-safety infrastructure such as blast chambers, explosion bunkers, or dedicated detonation cells.
- For an organic-peroxides and metal-alkyls lens, Nouryon and Arkema are the most relevant China benchmarks; LANXESS is the strongest metal-alkyl-adjacent China R&D footprint among the named competitors; several smaller peroxide players have little or no public China R&D disclosure.

## 3. What The Public Record Does And Does Not Show

### What we can say with confidence

- Large-multinational chemical R&D investments in China are concentrated in Shanghai and the Yangtze River Delta.
- Publicly disclosed investment sizes are usually cumulative campus investments, not stand-alone lab-equipment CAPEX.
- The most specific capability descriptions in the source set are:
  - BASF: solids handling, polymer recycling, robotic lab automation, surface-cleaning labs.
  - Solvay: pilot hall, automation and robotics, application labs.
  - Clariant: multi-scale catalyst prep, characterization, analysis, and performance testing.
  - Nouryon: eight specialized labs in Shanghai plus dedicated organic-peroxides and metal-alkyl application capability in China.

### What we could not verify publicly

- No official source in this review explicitly disclosed blast chambers, explosion bunkers, or dedicated explosive-material test cells inside China R&D centers.
- No official source quantified CAPEX for Nouryon's 2025 China innovation-center projects.
- Several smaller peroxide competitors do not publicly disclose whether they maintain China-based R&D labs at all.
